Warning

 A picture I made for a mini-album by FireWater. I modeled the box and the sign in 3D and I recolored it to remove the shadows and give it the right color. The exclamation mark and the stripes pattern were made in vector and added to the models as textures.
 The idea was to show the work-in-progress aspect of the mini-album by giving it this warning aspect and color. I think I was inspired by something at the television, the minimalist 3D feel instantly inspired me, and I think I’ll try to make other pictures like this in the future.
