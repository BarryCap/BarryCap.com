Forme

A picture I created using Blender. It makes part of the series Points de Lumière.
 The main idea of this scene was to stage a randomly generated shape, using the Random Object Generator. As usual in the Points de Lumière series, a color is chosen, to give a special atmosphere to the scene. I chose a light pink, as it is a non-realistic color for what a submarine scrutinizing the abyssal ground could ever see.
