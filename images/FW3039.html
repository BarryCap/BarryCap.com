FW3039

 An artwork I made using Blender (for the 3D FW logo and the background) and GIMP (for the colors). The image was made for a FireWater music which was originally named ‘ACID’ (it was realised on ABSOWW under the name ‘Compromettre son Âme’). This picture is part of the series FW----.
 The FireWater logo is presented in 3D to show the perspective of the sounds, the depth of the stereo effects, the deepness of the concept of a Goa Trance song inspired by the works of Infected Mushroom, by interviews of Michel Legrand about the sense of music, by the oriental machine which expends in aim of taking all over the musical concept of this track. The bright and wavy colors of this picture try to depict an idea of delirium which takes place in both artworks.
