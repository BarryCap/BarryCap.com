Bulle d'Eau

 A picture I made on Blender. It makes part of the series Points de Lumière.
 I was inspired by nightly seas, with this atmosphere of calm, meditating flow; light splashes of water that make one dream. The lighthouse represents a landmark in the middle of the ocean, a light… the point de lumière that keeps the world lit, preventing the barque sailor, the adventurer from getting lost in this dark scene. Because in real it is a scene, a bubble, une bulle that contains these elements. A spherical jail, aiming in making a pretty picture.
