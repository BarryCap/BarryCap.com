‘Who are you?’—The being asks.
‘Where do you come from?’
You are truly shocked by the way the being talks to you.
Her voice was incredibly… human.
First, you thought that it came from somewhere else,
but a quick look around made you realize that you were all alone.
‘Hello?’—You say, with a very shy voice.
‘You don’t know who you are, isn’t that right?’—She tells you.

Yes, it’s true. No! My name is Charles!
