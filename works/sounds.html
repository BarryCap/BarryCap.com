Sound

I always had interest in music, and I want to become a true musician.

25 Squares is a virtual musical instrument I worked on with the programming help of HLI. It features eight sound banks, recording, transposition and five random notes players. The synthesizer allows the user to play five octaves in the minor pentatonic scale, and any note can be played using the transposition panel. It works best on Chromium-based browsers, but now also works in Firefox.
